RegexForAccordance

Search Bible Texts in Accordance with Regular Expressions

Accordance is a popular Bible study program for the Mac (and iOS and Windows too). It is host to many high quality original language manuscripts and Bible translations. RegexForAccordance allows you to search any of the text modules in Accordance using regular expressions.

System Requirements

  • Mac OSX 10.9 or later
  • Accordance 10.4.5 or later
